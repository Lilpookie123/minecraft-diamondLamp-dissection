MSRP and Street Prices

Originally priced at $39.99, the Minecraft Diamond Lamp was well within reach of the average Minecraft fan, offering them an affordable piece of the Minecraft universe. Since its initial release, the price has fluctuated based on availability and demand. During holiday seasons or Minecraft anniversaries, the street price can climb to $60 or even higher due to increased demand. Currently, it can be found on resale websites like Amazon and eBay, typically ranging between $30 to $50, depending on condition and rarity.

Due to its collectible nature, limited edition versions have occasionally appeared, fetching premium prices. For example, the 2020 limited release with a special "glow-in-the-dark" feature retailed at $49.99 but now sells for up to $80 on collector's sites.

Retail Outlets

Originally sold through major retailers like Target, Walmart, and specialty gaming shops such as GameStop, the Minecraft Diamond Lamp found its way into homes across the world. Today, the best way to get your hands on one is through online platforms. Amazon remains a primary source for both new and used units, and eBay often offers listings from private collectors. Occasionally, local gaming conventions and pop culture fairs also have vendors selling rare and collectible editions of this lamp.

For those who want the full Minecraft shopping experience, Mojang's official merchandise store sometimes features this item, particularly during promotional periods or Minecraft events.

While there aren't many official rebranded versions, there are plenty of knock-offs and replicas. There have also been a few collaborations where the lamp was part of a bundle, such as during promotional releases with other Minecraft-themed merchandise, like posters or exclusive figurines.
